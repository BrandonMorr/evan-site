Evan Allan

Evan​ ​Allan​ ​is​ ​an​ ​emotionally​ ​provocative​ ​singer​ ​songwriter​ ​from​ ​Charlottetown,​ ​Prince Edward​ ​Island.​ ​Featuring​ ​energetic​ ​acoustic ​pop​ ​melodies​ ​paired​ ​with​ ​agile​ ​vocals,​ ​his dynamic​ ​range​ ​and​ ​lyricism​ ​can​ ​animate​ ​the​ ​dance​ ​floor​ ​only​ ​to​ ​bring​ ​it​ ​down​ ​to​ ​a​ ​whisper so​ ​you’re​ ​left​ ​with​ ​a​ ​smile​ ​and​ ​lump​ ​in​ ​your​ ​throat.​ ​He​ ​brings​ ​an​ ​anthology​ ​of​ ​personal experiences​ ​to​ ​life​ ​with​ ​clean​ ​tones​ ​and​ ​lyrics​ ​you​ ​might​ ​catch​ ​yourself​ ​singing​ ​in​ ​the shower.​ ​The​ ​songwriter​ ​has​ ​performed​ ​as​ ​a​ ​solo​ ​act​ ​and​ ​with​ ​his​ ​self​ ​titled​ ​band,​ ​drawing from​ ​influences​ ​like​ ​Gavin​ ​James​ ​and​ ​City​ ​and​ ​Colour.​ ​He’s​ diving ​head​ ​first​ ​into​ ​writing​ ​a demo​ ​release​ ​and​ ​a​ ​brand​ ​new​ ​wave​ ​of​ ​performance​ ​material.​ ​He’s​ ​got​ ​great​ ​things​ ​on​ ​the horizon​ ​but​ ​don’t​ ​take​ ​our​ ​word​ ​for​ ​it.​ ​Check​ ​him​ ​out!
